Lisa Fricci

Manager

Being in hospitality from the age of fourteen, Lisa worked in various restaurants and four star hotels. Working at the Woxfyn for over three years, Lisa worked her way up from a supervisor level to Restaurant Manager.

Hank Kalani

Bar Director

Hank Kalani is an award winning mixologist. He has been with the Woxfyn since 2018 and has worked his way through some of the most prestigious cocktail bars in the world.

Mic Matsu

Head Chef

Mic Matsu is a devoted food lover. But most importantly he is the talented head chef and owner of the Woxfyn. Mic has always been most passionate about cooking with real food; real flavour and making the most of the fresh seasonal produce.

Vanessa Marino

Wine Director

Vanessa Marino brings an exceptional depth of wine knowledge and fine wine to Woxfyn. Since her arrival, Woxfyn has established itself as an award-winning restaurant channeling and fusing high-quality cuisine alongside a World of Fine Wine three-star wine list.
